# Customer Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes consumer shopping behavior to uncover purchasing trends, customer preferences, and key business insights. The objective is to help stakeholders understand customer engagement patterns, optimize marketing strategies, and improve overall business performance through data-driven decision-making.

The project combines Python for data preparation, PostgreSQL for analytical querying, and Power BI for interactive dashboard development.

---

## Business Problem

A leading retail company wants to better understand customer shopping behavior in order to improve sales, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty. The company is interested in identifying how demographics, product categories, subscription status, ratings, and purchasing patterns influence consumer decisions.

### Key Question

How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?

---

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Preparation & Cleaning (Python)

* Imported and explored the dataset
* Checked for missing values and duplicates
* Corrected data types and formatting issues
* Prepared data for analysis and visualization

### 2. Data Analysis (PostgreSQL)

* Created and managed the database
* Wrote SQL queries to analyze:

  * Customer distribution
  * Revenue by category
  * Average purchase amount
  * Review ratings
  * Subscription behavior
  * Payment preferences
  * Product performance

### 3. Dashboard Development (Power BI)

Built an interactive dashboard featuring:

* Average Purchase Amount KPI
* Average Review Rating KPI
* Total Customers KPI
* Revenue by Category
* Sales by Category
* Subscription Status Distribution
* Interactive Filters and Slicers

## Key Findings

* Clothing generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
* Average customer purchase amount was approximately $59.76.
* Average customer review rating was 3.75.
* Most customers were non-subscribers, indicating opportunities to improve loyalty program adoption.
* Product categories contributed differently to revenue and sales performance.
* Customer purchasing patterns can be leveraged for targeted marketing campaigns.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Increase Customer Loyalty

* Promote subscription programs through exclusive discounts and rewards.
* Encourage repeat purchases through personalized offers.

### Focus on High-Performing Categories

* Increase inventory and marketing efforts for top-performing categories.
* Expand product offerings within high-revenue segments.

### Improve Customer Experience

* Monitor customer ratings and feedback.
* Address issues associated with lower-rated products.

### Implement Personalized Marketing

* Segment customers based on purchasing behavior.
* Deliver tailored promotions and recommendations.
